My podcast recommendations:

General interest

  • This American Life
  • Radiolab
  • Invisibilia: I love how they explore all sides of an issue and take a very empathetic approach to reporting.
  • On the Media
  • Undiscovered

Spirituality

  • On Being: Amazing conversations with spiritual leaders.

Design

99 percent invisible: explores the design of lots of things you probably never thought about.

Storytelling

Nonfictional

  • The Moth story slam
  • The memory palace: moments in history, lushly narrated as stories
  • Storycorps
  • Radio diaries
  • Mortified: reading from childhood diaries.

Fictional

  • Night Vale: Fictional radio show in a strange small town, full of conspiracies.
  • The truth
  • Off-Book: Improvised musical.

Mythical

Lore

Philosophy

  • Hi-Phi Nation: Turns philosophy into stories. Raises deep, meaningful questions.
  • The Philosopher’s Zone: Accessible tidbits on philosophy

Science and Technology

  • Quanta: High-quality reporting on advances in math and science.
  • Reply All: All things to do with the web.
  • Review the future: Futurist podcast, explores the impact of technology on everything.

Speculative

  • Imaginary worlds: About creating fictional worlds & “what makes us suspend our disbelief”.
  • Flash forward pod: Ask “what if” questions about the future. Great inspiration for science fiction!

Writing

  • Writing Excuses: Great fiction-writing advice. Each year focuses on a different aspect of the craft: character, genre, plot, worldbuilding, etc.
  • Rationally writing: On writing rational fiction.

Language

  • The Allusionist: Wonderful wordplay.
  • Lexicon valley: A linguist answers your questions.
  • The world in words

People & relationships

  • Strangers, also here: Has some of the most honest emotional content I’ve heard in audio.
  • The heart
  • Love + Radio

Other

  • Rationally speaking
  • Freakonomics Radio
  • TED Radio hour
  • Reveal: Investigative reporting.
  • The Weeds
  • 80,000 hours: effective altruism
